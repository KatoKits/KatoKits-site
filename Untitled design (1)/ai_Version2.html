Pizza Shop Dramatic Play

  1. Activity Description:

    Children take on roles in a pretend pizza restaurant, practicing vocabulary, social skills, and simple math by building pizzas, taking orders, and delivering slices.

  2. Step-by-Step Instructions:
    1. Set up pizza shop with menus, toppings, and props.
    2. Introduce roles and vocabulary.
    3. Model taking an order and making a pizza.
    4. Let children rotate roles and complete tasks in pairs or groups.
  3. Demonstration:

    Model taking an order, preparing a pizza with felt pieces, and delivering it to a 'customer.' Show how to use polite phrases and count slices.

  4. Reflection & Sharing:
    • What was your favorite job today?
    • Did you use any new words?
    • What kind of pizza did you make?
    • How did it feel to work together?
  5. Developmental Checklist:
    • Engages in roleplay using themed vocabulary
    • Takes customer orders using complete sentences
    • Builds pretend pizza with fine motor precision
    • Uses counting skills to track slices or totals
    • Cooperates in shared dramatic play
    • Follows multi-step restaurant routines
    • Demonstrates empathy during role exchanges
    • Uses pretend writing to take notes or orders
    • Explains their choices during imaginative play
